About Us

Resolute Business Brokers is a nationwide business brokerage firm headquartered in Las Vegas, NV. The professionals at Resolute Business Brokers bring a diverse background in both asset-based and stock transactions, guiding the execution of business sales across industries. With over two decades of experience and involvement in more than a hundred transactions, our team has worked in the small and middle market from coast to coast — ranging from resort-based businesses to medical service companies. We take a hands-on approach to every engagement, carefully considering each seller’s goals and needs while crafting the best strategy and structure to achieve them. Every transaction presents monetary, legal, and legacy considerations; we address these complexities from the outset to deliver the best possible outcome for our clients in all respects.

Business valuations, confidential marketing, buyer vetting, negotiation support, and closing guidance — from listing to transfer of ownership.
