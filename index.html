About Us

Resolute Business Brokers is a nationwide business brokerage firm headquartered in Las Vegas, NV. The Professionals at Resolute Business Brokers have a diverse background in both asset based, and stock transactions in the execution of the sale of your business. We have been involved in over a hundred transactions over two decades of service in the small and middle market, ranging from resort-based businesses to medical service companies, from coast to coast. We take a very hands-on approach to each transaction and carefully consider the sellers wants and needs going into each relationship, guiding them to the best strategy and structure to achieve our clients’ goals. In each transaction there are a various of monetary, legal and legacy issues that present themselves and we work these parallels from the outset to achieve the best outcome for our client in all respects.
